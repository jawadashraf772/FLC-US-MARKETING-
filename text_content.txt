 How appointment setting works 
 We’ve mastered each step and gone deeper than other outsourced 
 appointment setting companies so you get ready-to-buy leads. 
Analysis and strategy development
To craft a unique strategy that will yield the highest impact, we:
Dive into your market, past sales performance, industry trends, competition, and goals
Research your sales and marketing assets, messaging, channels, and successful case studies
Map out the best engagement channels with relevant messaging approaches to connect with the right decision-makers
Finding and qualifying the right prospects
To ensure precise targeting, your dedicated researchers will:
Manually search, validate, and identify key decision-makers who fit your ideal customer profile
Filter out low-potential leads early so that your team only speaks with real opportunities
Segment and enrich contact lists to enable highly personalized outreach
Ensuring top-tier campaign performance
Before launching outreach, our email deliverability specialists:
Audit your domain health and email reputation to prevent deliverability issues
Optimize DNS settings (SPF, DMARC, DKIM, BIMI) and resolve blacklisting concerns if needed
Provide a content compliance guide to ensure emails bypass spam filters and land in inboxes
Prepare and set up the rest of the outreach infrastructure according to the selected channels
Cross-channel prospects engagement
Now, it’s the turn of our copywriters and SDRs who:
Strategize on messaging, craft emails and scripts that spark interest, tackle pain points, and start real conversations
Launch coordinated, multitouch outreach across email, phone, LinkedIn, and any other relevant outbound channels to maximize engagement
Track engagement in real time and adjust lead generation campaigns for better results
Turning interest into sales meetings
Once prospects show interest, our SDRs:
Hold direct conversations to qualify prospects and gauge their readiness to buy
Connect with engaged prospects and book meetings straight to your calendar
Send timely reminders and thoughtful follow-ups to reduce no-shows
Reengage leads who drop off and keep the momentum going
Tracking, optimizing, and scaling your results
Our team, led by a dedicated account manager, ensures 
 continuous growth by:
Monitoring every interaction to keep full visibility of performance and pipeline growth
Continuously refining strategies to boost conversions and ROI
Staying aligned with your team to ensure constant improvements and long-term success

 FAQ 
What pricing options do you provide?
Apart from our 
3 basic pricing packages
, we also create custom quotes tailored to your specific needs.
  To calculate your personalized appointment setting service cost, we consider factors like your market and company specifics, your target personas, the monthly lead and appointment quota your team can handle, your sales goals, the mix of channels we’ll need to implement to achieve your targets, and many others.
 Our account executives will help you choose the best strategy and budget.
What do we need to provide Belkins with to start our campaign?
To launch your campaign, we’ll need your marketing materials, successful campaign samples, and case studies to craft a compelling omnichannel strategy. Access to your CRM is also essential for tracking progress and optimizing performance in real time.
  Your CRM, databases, sales assets, and all sensitive information are fully protected under our NDA.
How exactly will you book meetings for us?
Your dedicated SDRs will operate as an extension of your team. We connect your calendar to scheduling tools like Calendly, giving SDRs full visibility into your availability. 
  Once a prospect agrees to meet, the SDR shares your calendar link with them to select a suitable time. As soon as the prospect picks a slot, the meeting is automatically added to your calendar — no back-and-forth needed. 
 To ensure the meeting actually happens, our SDRs stay in close contact with each prospect. They send timely reminders, follow up as the meeting approaches, and proactively reschedule if anything changes on either side.
What happens after the appointment is booked?
Once the meeting is scheduled, we keep the momentum going. SDRs send timely reminders to prospects — usually 24 hours and 1 hour before the call — and monitor their responses to reduce no-shows. 
  If a prospect becomes unresponsive or needs to reschedule, our team steps in immediately to reengage and secure a new time. 
  On your side, you’ll receive a short meeting brief in advance. This includes key prospect info like their company background, pain points discussed, and why they agreed to the call, so your sales team walks in prepared. 
  After the meeting, we follow up with you to gather feedback and make real-time improvements to the outreach strategy based on what’s converting best.
Can you work with our CRM?
Yes, we can work with your existing CRM to keep lead information up to date and ensure full visibility into booked appointments and campaign progress.
 That said, the level of integration depends on the tools you use. For most CRMs, we can only support basic functions. If you use HubSpot (or decide to 
switch to HubSpot
 from your old platform), we can offer deeper integration and automate more of the process for smoother collaboration.
  No matter what tools you’re using, your account manager will work closely with your team to align workflows and keep everything running efficiently.
How exactly do you integrate into our sales and marketing teams?
We adapt our approach based on who owns appointment setting and omnichannel GTM motions. 
  If marketing owns appointment setting, our potential focus could include integrating CRM and pipeline data, aligning sales metrics with marketing performance, and analyzing cross-functional dynamics to improve overall revenue impact.
  If your outbound function is owned by sales, then we will prioritize integrating with and involving your marketing team. Our potential focus could include introducing social selling approaches, expanding the number of channels considered in lead nurturing, setting up priorities within potential customer segments, aligning promotional efforts with lead qualification criteria, or integrating other marketing channels owned by your in-house marketing team.
How will you maintain our brand style and tone of voice in messaging?
We thoroughly analyze your marketing and sales materials to capture your brand’s voice. On top of that, each project is handled by experienced content strategists and editors who ensure messaging consistency and accuracy.
Who owns the leads you find for us?
You own all the leads we find for you, and we outline this in our agreements for full transparency and peace of mind.
How do you qualify the leads that you find for us?
Our research team carefully selects leads based on your ideal customer profile and buyer personas. We verify their contact details and unique data points to ensure precision.
  Each lead undergoes both automated validation and manual quality checks following our QA protocols. Once finalized, we send the list to you for approval before outreach begins.
